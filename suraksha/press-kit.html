About Suraksha

Suraksha is a secure, anonymous platform based in Jansla, Punjab, designed to help survivors of abuse report incidents and connect with a network of verified counselors and NGOs. Our mission is to leverage technology to break the cycle of silence and provide a safe first step towards healing.
