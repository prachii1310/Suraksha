The Power of Anonymous Reporting: Why It Matters

Posted on July 30, 2025 by Anjali Mehta

For survivors of abuse, the fear of retaliation or judgment can be a significant barrier to seeking help. This is where the power of anonymous reporting becomes a critical tool for empowerment and safety.

By removing the requirement of identity, we allow individuals to share their story and seek initial help without immediate risk. It is the first, crucial step on a long road to recovery, and at Suraksha, we are committed to making that first step as safe as possible.
