Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our platform and process.

Is my report truly anonymous?

Yes. We are committed to your privacy. You are not required to provide any personally identifiable information unless you choose to for follow-up purposes. Our platform is engineered to protect your anonymity.

What happens after I submit a report?

Once submitted, your report is securely transmitted to a verified NGO or counselor in our network. If you create an account, you can track the status (e.g., "Pending Review," "Counselor Assigned") on your personal dashboard.

Is this a government service?

No, Suraksha is an independent platform developed by volunteers and professionals. We work alongside official channels and support organizations to provide an alternative, secure reporting avenue.

Who will see my report?

Only trained and vetted professionals who are assigned to your case will have access to the details of your report. All our partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
